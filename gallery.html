Yoga Pictures

This picture shows a very relaxing pose. This is pose is Child's Pose along with an amazing shoulder opener. If you thread your arm under your body and lay the rest of your body weight onto that arm you will feel an amazing stretch in your shoulder.

In this photo, since we were practicing on surf boards, we started off with modified poses. This pose is a modification of three legged dog. In the regular pose the yogi would be on their hands and balls of their feet with either leg up in the air.

Continued from the above picture.

This photo shows the modified dancers pose. Dancer's pose usually consists of one foot grounded on the mat, one hand in the air and the other catching the foot in the air. This is a great hamstring stretch.

This photo shows the amazing downward dog. This pose is practiced throughout the whole flow. It is a great chest, calf, hamstring, foot, almost everything stretch. It allows for relaxation as well as strengthing.

This pose is also a great hamstring opener. It is usually taken before the amazing hip opener, half pigeon. It allows for a deep stretch that most people don't get on a daily or even weekly basis.

This beautiful picture shows the amazing bridge pose. Bridge is a great abdominal opener as well as body strengthener.
